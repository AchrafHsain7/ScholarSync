About Us

Research Website: ScholarSync

Scholar-sync is a web application designed to provide a collaborative space for research and surveys. It makes it easier for researchers to connect with others in their field, conduct surveys, and receive real-time results. Users can communicate with fellow researchers through direct messaging or posting, engage in discussions, seek guidance, and contribute to ongoing research projects.Our app offers a unique educational experience by creating a vibrant community, allowing users to witness and actively contribute to the research projects of others. Additionally, they can acquire help and advice to enhance their own research projects.
